## Decision

The joint model's age Gaussian process is **disabled by default**
(`use_age_gp=False`). A new flag on `build_joint_model` exposes this
setting; with the flag off, the shared `mu_A` HSGP and all eight
outcome-specific `delta_A_k` HSGPs are omitted from the PyMC graph.
The flag can be turned back on for a sensitivity fit via
`ModelSpec.extra={"use_age_gp": True, ...}`.

Combined with the earlier LKJ-residual drop
(`notes/202604181630-lrp55-lkj-drop-and-comparison-fix.md`), LRP55 is
now a purely linear Beta-Binomial over eight outcomes with shared
predictor structure.
