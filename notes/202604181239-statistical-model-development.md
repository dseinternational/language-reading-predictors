## Other open questions — resolved from the data

- **Age range**: 62–120 months at baseline (mean 82, sd 15.6 months).
- **Sample size per arm**: 28 control, 26 intervention; 54 children
  total. After dropping one baseline-incomplete row in the ITT phase,
  28 control + 25 intervention = 53 children enter LRP52–LRP55.
- **LRP55 identifiability concern**: 28 free off-diagonal correlations
  in Σ with 53 children per outcome — LKJCorr(η=4) is used to shrink
  toward zero, but the posterior should be inspected for
  prior-domination of specific pairs before interpreting.
- **Missingness**: ≤ 2 % of pre-scores missing across measures. Drop-
  with-warning suffices; no imputation pass needed for this iteration.
- **DAG decision on B in LRP58**: follow the plan's best guess — B is
  a descendant of L in the literacy-development cascade and is *not*
  conditioned on. Rationale is documented prominently in
  `docs/models/lrp58/index.qmd`.

## Key design choices

- **Shared factory**: LRP52/53/54 are a single `build_itt_model`
  parameterised only by `outcome_symbol`. Specification drift across
  ITT models is therefore impossible by construction.
- **Non-centred LKJ**: LRP55 uses `pm.LKJCholeskyCov(eta=4)` with a
  non-centred `z_raw ~ Normal(0, 1)` and `u = z_raw @ (sigma[:,None] *
  chol).T`, avoiding the MvNormal funnel geometry.
- **Age GP partial pooling (LRP55)**: `f_A_k(a) = mu_A(a) +
  delta_A_k(a)` with `delta_A_k` amplitude ~ HalfNormal(0.3). Fallback
  to independent per-outcome age GPs is a flag
  (`partial_pool_age_gp=False`).
- **Mechanism curve extraction** pulls `f_mech` posterior draws,
  orders by `logit_safe(mechanism_post)`, and writes both a PNG and
  `mechanism_curve.csv` per mechanism run.
- **Row subsetting returned from factories**: `BuiltModel.prepared`
  exposes the actually-used prepared data so the pipeline can align
  posterior indices to input rows (factories may drop rows with
  missing post-scores).
