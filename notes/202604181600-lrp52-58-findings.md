### β_G in mechanism models is NOT a direct-effect estimate

All three mechanism models produce β_G posterior means near zero
(LRP56 −0.04, LRP57 −0.03, LRP58 +0.02). **Do not read this as
"the treatment effect on W is fully mediated."** The mechanism models
pool all three phases, but in phase 1 (t2 → t3) group 2 is receiving
the intervention and group 1 is in maintenance; in phase 2 (t3 → t4)
both arms are post-intervention. β_G is the coefficient on the
baseline group-assignment indicator, which averages over phases in
which both arms have received treatment. A near-zero β_G is expected
regardless of mediation structure.

Proper ITT-phase-only mediation decomposition (NDE / NIE) is deferred
to LRP59+ as planned in the original design.

## Integrated picture

1. The intervention **raises word reading (W)** by ≈ 0.41 logit units
   (≈ 1.7 pp on probability scale at sample-mean baseline).
2. It **raises letter-sound knowledge (L) even more**, by ≈ 0.67
   logit units — the biggest effect in the joint model.
3. Blending (B) is suggestive of a smaller positive effect.
4. Receptive and expressive vocabulary (R, E), receptive grammar (T),
   basic concept knowledge (F) and phonetic spelling (P) show **no
   credible effect at 95 %** in these data.
5. Letter-sound knowledge is **independently associated with word
   reading** (LRP58: f_mech amplitude CI excludes zero; mean slope
   ≈ +0.14 per logit unit). Expressive vocabulary adds independent
   contribution (γ_E = +0.28 credibly positive in LRP58 conditioned
   set).
6. The mechanism picture is compatible with L being the main causal
   pathway by which the intervention lifts W, but formal mediation
   decomposition is out of scope for this phase.
