  baseline (1.274). Tuning redistributed signal massively away

- **`trog` at rank 2 (0.037)** ‚Äî a language / grammar measure.
  The first predictor beyond the base level is grammar, not
  vocabulary ‚Äî interesting given the target is a vocabulary gain.
- **`attend` at rank 5 (0.019)** ‚Äî intervention attendance
  appears as meaningful, consistent with LRP01 / LRP05 findings.
- **`time` at 0 importance** ‚Äî unusual. In LRP01 / LRP03 / LRP05
